Get Started Our Start-Ups

NIDUS is dedicated to promote innovation and Entrepreneurship.

The Incubator facility for start-ups of college students started in 2017 to provide a platform for future college students to become entrepreneurs and grow their ideas into full-blown business units. Since then successful startups have emerged from NIDUS like Garniche, Learning Folks, Craving For Gaming and many more.

  • To produce successful firms that will leave the program financially viable and free-standing.
  • To produce job creators instead of job seekers who can commercialize new technologies, and strengthen national economies.
  • To provide business and technical assistance to the Incubator tenants along with official affiliation with the incubator.
  • A supportive community with an entrepreneurial environment.
  • A direct link to entrepreneurs, immediate networking and commercial opportunities with other tenant firms.

More Start-Ups

Some more start-ups nurtured by NIDUS.

Neem Tree Agro Solutions Pvt. Ltd.

Founded in 2021 by Dhiresh Kumar, Paras Jain, and Nishant Chauhan, aims to revolutionize traditional agriculture in India. With NT-Kisan, the agrotech startup addresses the lack of knowledge among rural farmers. NT-Kisan, a data-driven crop enhancement app, analyzes factors like soil type and weather predictions to provide personalized recommendations.

Sparko India: Sparks for Technologies

Sparko India was established by Abhay Kumar Mishra in 2017, Delhi. He is an Instrumentation and Control Engineer who has been working in the field of Analytical Instrumentation. Sparko India is a service provider of Food and Beverage, Oil Refinery and Pharma Industry. The company aims to design and install complete systems for monitoring and controlling industrial plants.

Proffus

Proffus is a niche Technology Solutions Provider, offering a broad range of smart services and solutions in Application Development, Website Development, Digital Marketing and Graphic Designing. Their core strength is their team of competent and experienced professionals, who go above and beyond to deliver top quality work product.

Board Easy

In 2019, Gaurav and Shailesh faced the language barrier with English and decided to solve the problem for people facing such issues. Introducing a platform to learn in your regional language, get online classes in your mother tongue. At Board Easy, helping an individual understand better.
